## 1. Company & Product Overview

**Sydecar** is a software-first platform for SPV and fund formation and administration. It is not a marketplace — GPs retain their LP relationships. Products: SPVs, Syndicate, API (Fund+ has been transitioned to a specialist partner and is no longer a core product offering).

**Core differentiators:**
- 4-hour SPV review
- Software-first automation (not a law firm, not a manual administrator)
- Secondary transaction support
- Transparent pricing: 2% fee, $4,500–$12,500; no carry, no marketplace model
- Privacy (GPs retain LP relationships)

**Authorized spokespersons:** Nik Talreja (CEO), Shri (COO), Halle Kaplan-Allen (Head of Marketing)

## 2. 2026 Strategic Positioning

### The Shift
Sydecar is transitioning from "fast and cost-effective for early-adopter emerging managers" to **"the clear market leader for institutional-grade venture SPVs."**

This means winning on **trust, reliability, and institutional credibility** — not price or speed alone. Marketing should make Sydecar feel like premium infrastructure, not a scrappy upstart.

### The Mandate: Be Bolder
| Principle | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Lead with conviction | Form and communicate clear positions on how the industry should operate |
| Shape narratives proactively | Don't wait to react; publish our perspective first |
| Make bigger bets | A hosted SPV conference > four community happy hours |
| Raise the bar on collaboration | Every partner, panelist, sponsor should give us brand lift with our ICP |
| Compete with confidence | Use marketing to punch up at competitors cleanly and directly |
| Invest ahead of the product | Brand presence creates opportunities the product follows into |

### Two H1 Company Priorities (that marketing is directly anchored to)
1. **Deliver best-in-class venture SPV experience** — protect and fuel the referral engine; ensure product and experience are unambiguously best-in-class for an increasingly sophisticated customer base
2. **Establish Sydecar in institutional private markets** — begin building presence with BDs and RIAs as the distribution channel for private market assets reaches HNW/retail investors

## 3. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: Emerging Fund Managers (Fund 2)
- **Stage:** Fund 2
- **Team size:** 3–5 people
- **Fund size:** $30M–$50M
- **Deal volume:** 8–12 SPVs/year
- **Core need:** Fast, repeatable SPV execution; institutional-grade ops without a full back office
- **Key message:** "We make you look great to your LPs. Get your deal done smoothly, on time."

### Secondary ICP #1: Institutional Managers
- **Stage:** Fund 4+
- **Team size:** 10+ investors, full ops team
- **AUM:** $250M–$500M
- **Deal volume:** 2–8 SPVs/year
- **Core need:** High compliance, customization, institutional reporting, scale
- **Key message:** "Manage SPVs with ease and at scale. Give your finance team leverage."

### Secondary ICP #2: Regulated Wealth Channels (BD/RIA) **[IN PROGRESS / TBD]**
- **Who:** Broker-dealers, RIAs, wealth platforms
- **Core need:** SPV distribution to wealth channels; regulatory compliance
- **Key message:** Educational — teaching these audiences to use SPVs as conduits for private market access
- **Status:** Demand-testing mode in H1 2026. Firmographic details still being refined through customer discovery. Do not treat this segment as fully defined.

### Key ICP Notes
- The primary ICP reflects an **evolution** — these are managers who have graduated from Fund 1 and demand institutional-grade execution
- ICP is designed to inform marketing strategy and product roadmap, not restrict which customers we accept
- Competitors: AngelList (marketplace model, no secondaries support, higher setup cost), Carta (higher annual cost, no secondaries, fund admin not core focus). Sydecar wins on speed, pricing, privacy, and secondary support.

## 6. Brand Voice (Non-Negotiable)

- **Tone:** Authoritative, trustworthy, confident, elegant
- **Voice rules:** Active voice. No em dashes. No contractions in formal documents. No contrastive framing ("Unlike competitors..." or "While others..."). No AI vocabulary (transformative, seamless, game-changing, robust, etc.)
- **Channel calibration:** Formal for blog/long-form; slightly warmer for LinkedIn; concise and direct for email
- **Content principle:** The manager's decisions, outcomes, and credibility are the focus. Sydecar is the enabler in the background — not the hero.

For detailed brand voice rules, invoke the `sydecar-brand-voice` skill.

## 10. Key Regulatory & Industry Context

- **FIPVCC / SB 54 / SB 164 (California):** New California regulation affecting SPV operators. Sydecar has published a help center article and blog post. A formal letter to DFPI requesting consolidated reporting guidance and a deadline extension is being finalized. This is compliance-critical; do not produce content that mischaracterizes the regulatory requirements.
- **Layered SPVs:** Sensitive topic following negative market sentiment and a Bloomberg article on SPV risks. Sydecar has repositioned around responsible execution and industry standards, with a sponsor attestation feature. Messaging should emphasize institutional rigor, not defensiveness.
- **Fund+ transition:** Sydecar transitioned its Fund+ business to a specialist partner (Zive). Fund+ references should be removed from all active marketing materials and the website. Do not create new Fund+ content.

- Anything targeting the BD/RIA segment beyond awareness/educational content (product not yet defined; demand still being tested)
